The Barbell Squat

A God among excersise

8X Mr Olympia winner Ronnie Coleman performing the barbell back squat

The squat is considered by many to be the ultimate test of strength, technique and will.

It also serves as a reassuring metaphor for life, having a heavy load placed upon your shoulders and finding the strength to push through it no matter how hard it may be when you hit bottom. Great for quadriceps hypertrophy.

Unfortunately, the squat has recieved some backlash due to the ill concieved notion that it is inherently bad for your knees. It is not.
